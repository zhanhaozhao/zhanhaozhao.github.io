I am currently a Research Fellow in the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore. I work on Database Systems, advised by Prof. OOI Beng Chin.

I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science from Renmin University of China in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. DU Xiaoyong and Prof LU Wei. I was a visiting PhD student in National Univeristy of Singapore, supervised by Prof. OOI Beng Chin. I received my Bachelor’s degree from Zhongnan University of Economic and Law. I also worked in Tencent as a Research Intern from 2017 to 2023.
