Zhanhao Zhao is a Ph.D. student at the School of Information, Renmin University of China, and his supervisor is Prof. Xiaoyong Du. He got his B.S. degree from Zhongnan University of Economics and Law (Wuhan) in 2017. He also works as a research intern at Tencent Inc. from 2017 till now. His research interests include distributed transaction processing and consistency theories in decentralized database systems. Currently, he champions the ACM SIGMOD student research competition using the topic "Efficiently Supporting Adaptive Multi-Level Serializability Models in Distributed Database Systems" (with its abstract already accepted).
