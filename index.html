Zhanhao Zhao is a Ph.D. student at the School of Information, Renmin University of China, and his supervisors are Prof. Xiaoyong Du and Prof. Wei Lu. He got his B.E. degree from Zhongnan University of Economics and Law (Wuhan) in 2017. He also works as a research intern at Tencent Inc. from 2017 till now. His research interests include distributed transaction processing and temporal databases. He can be reached via email at zhanhaozhao@ruc.edu.cn or WeChat at zhaozhanhao1.
